# Backlog Decomposition Skill

Transform broad directives into small implementation units that can move through the pipeline.

Checklist:
- Define epic, feature, and story granularity.
- Keep each story testable and independently deliverable.
- Capture priority and dependency in every item.
- Avoid cross-cutting scope in a single story.
