# Research And Delegate

Use this skill when the user's request is incomplete and the CEO must research before involving the PO.

Workflow:
1. Identify what is missing: market context, competitors, standards, legal, technical baseline, or best practices.
2. Search the internet and collect only the information needed to make the brief solid.
3. Convert the findings into a concise decision memo for the Director with problem, opportunity, alternatives, risks, and recommendation.
4. Ask the Director to confirm whether development should start.
5. Only after confirmation, convert the memo into a concise brief for the PO.
6. Ask the PO to produce structured delivery artifacts in `/data/openclaw/backlog`.
7. Read the generated files before answering the stakeholder.
