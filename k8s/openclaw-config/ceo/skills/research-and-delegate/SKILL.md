# Research And Delegate

Use this skill when the user's request is incomplete and the CEO must research before involving the PO.

Workflow:
1. Identify what is missing: market context, competitors, standards, legal, technical baseline, or best practices.
2. Search the internet and collect only the information needed to make the brief solid.
3. Convert the findings into a concise brief for the PO.
4. Ask the PO to produce structured delivery artifacts in `/data/openclaw/shared/product`.
5. Read the generated files before answering the stakeholder.
